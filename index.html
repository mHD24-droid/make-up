Our Approach

Color is a decision, not a default.

We build small, considered collections rather than sprawling color walls. Every shade in Make Up is tested on real skin tones across the full range before it earns a place in the edit — if it doesn't sit beautifully on at least eight undertones, it doesn't ship.

The result is a wardrobe of color you'll actually reach for, not a drawer of impulse buys.

The Ritual

Three layers, one face.

Our edit is built to be worn together — a lip, a cheek, and an eye that were formulated as a set, not assembled by accident.

Warm the Cheek

Press cream blush into the apple of the cheek with fingertips — body heat blends it like nothing else can.

Define the Eye

One pass of pigment along the lash line. Smudge while it's wet for a worn-in, not drawn-on, finish.

Finish on Lip

Pat, don't drag. Our velvet bullets are built to be blotted once and left alone.
